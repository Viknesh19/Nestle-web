Is breakfast cereal healthy?

Breakfast is part of a healthy and balanced diet, and Nestlé® Breakfast Cereals are both nutritious and tasty with:
• 100% of our cereals with “Wholegrain as No.1 Ingredient”
• 100% of the portfolio with source of fibre
• 80% of our cereals contain at least 4 vitamins and 2 minerals.
• 100% of our cereals have equal to or less than 2 teaspoons of sugar (~10g) per serving
• 100% of our cereals have less than ⅛ teaspoon of salt (~250mg sodium) per serving
